Interests

I believe we all picked up a camera at a very young age, no matter how basic that camera may have been and considering we live in a World with smartphones. My first camera was actually a phone camera, the Nokia N95. I remember working during school holidays and saved half of the price, and my parents chipping in the other half, to buy the phone. This was the birth of a passion that I don't think I will ever let go.

Scenery Photos

Sometimes you find yourself taking a moment to appreciate the spectacular views in front of you when nature stops you dead in your tracks. It can be the dawn or dusk of a day, a fresh patch of flowers with a mountain range in the background, or a waterfall. There is no better model and poser than nature for your camera lens.

Food photos

Have you ever seen a photo of a salad, steak or a brownie with chocolate flowing freely as you slice it? I'm no professional food photographer but I always love capturing those delicious moments. These are memories that will remind you of the delicious food that you've had the pleasure of enjoying.

Architectural Photography

Architectural photography is about the interior and exterior design of building and structures. From spooky looking houses to modern architecture, it's always a pleasure looking at the designs, the placements, the location and everything that comes with making an architecture eye pleasing.

Still Life photos

Still life photography is about capturing inanimate objects whether natural or manmade. You can use any object, imagine a scene and lighting to create a still life photo. Object selection, arrangement, and lighting are the key components to getting a good still life photo.
